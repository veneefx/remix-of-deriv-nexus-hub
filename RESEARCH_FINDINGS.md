# Reference Site Analysis: dtnexusapp.com

## Design Specifications
- **Background Color**: `rgb(20, 20, 20)` (Dark theme)
- **Primary Accent Color**: `rgb(228, 31, 40)` (Red)
- **Secondary Accent Color**: `rgb(0, 212, 255)` (Light Blue)
- **Fonts**: 
  - Body: Poppins
  - Headings: Open Sans
- **Layout**: 
  - Header with sticky navigation.
  - Hero section with centered text and background glow.
  - "Trade On Our Next-Gen Trading Platform" section with rocket image and feature list.
  - "Our Platform" section with stats (12K+ Active Traders, 99.9% Uptime, etc.) and feature cards.
  - "Centralized Account Management" section with dashboard links.
  - "Why Trade With DTNexus" section with benefit list.
  - "Trading Reimagined" section with large image and text.
  - "Comprehensive Currency Market Tracker" (TradingView widget integration).
  - "Join a Growing Community" section with social media integration (Instagram/Discord).
  - FAQ section with accordion.
  - Footer with multi-column links and social icons.
